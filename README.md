# Paradise Nursery

Paradise Nursery is a premium online destination for houseplant enthusiasts. We offer a curated selection of high-quality plants, ranging from air-purifying giants to low-maintenance succulents, all handpicked to bring serenity and life into your living space.

## Features
- **Landing Page**: An elegant introduction to our nursery.
- **Product Listing**: Browse over 6 unique plants categorized for your convenience.
- **Shopping Cart**: Manage your selections with ease, including quantity adjustments and real-time total calculations.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for all devices.

## Tech Stack
- React
- Redux Toolkit
- Lucide React (Icons)
- Vite
